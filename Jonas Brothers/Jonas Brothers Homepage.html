The Jonas Brothers

The Jonas Brothers are a pop boy band. The band has 3 members, who are brothers. Their names are Kevin, Joe, and Nick. Here is a link to their official website. Jonas Brothers.com The Jonas Brothers formed their band in 2005, and quickly became successful. They released their first album; It's About Time in 2006. In 2013 they broke up as a band. Nick started his own solo career in music, and Joe went joined a band called DNCE, and also started to make some of his own music. They started off as young teenagers, and are now adults. In 2019, they reformed as a band, and released a a song named Sucker. A little while after this they announced a new album: Happiness Begins, followed by a world tour.
